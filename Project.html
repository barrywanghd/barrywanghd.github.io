Scope & Solution & Schedule

Project Scope

Project was divided into 3 steps of 10 sub-projects in order to lower down the total complexity.

Solution Scope

Yellow area were new sub-systems integrated with surrounding live system from E2E perspective.

Project Schedule

Schedule was based on the sub-projects and combined the overall control.

Organization & Way of Working

High Level Organization

Total project manager, subproject managers and solution architects were set up accordingly.

Working level Organization

Overall test leader and group leaders were setup accordingly and run in an self-organized way.

Way of Working

This was defined by working level team itself through brainstorming and bottom-up approach .

Monitoring & Control

Progress One Page

This page included the dashboard, progress of each subproject and Percent Of Completion.

Schedule tracking

The schedule of planned, actual and forecasted was consolidated in one slide on high level.

Project finance

EVA report reflected the project progress from the financial factors .

Change Control

Change Tracking Log

The status of changes were tracked timely including all the major aspects.

CCB Meeting

Pre-CCB and CCB meeting were held periodically or on demand.

CCB Decision

The decision of CCB meeting was recorded and followed up.

Collaboration

Project Portal

This portal was based on Sharepoint including all necessary info for team and the major progress.

Major Columns

The portal was structured by the areas which was quite easy for team to locate the info .

Issue tracking

Once the list was updated by one of members. It would be automatically published to web site.

Acceptance & Handover

Acceptance

Acceptance was based on a number of milestones and per subproject.

Handover

The workshop to transfer knowledge, document preparation and warranty info were underway.
